The Ceanian Kingdom and Theocracy of Thudia are in a state of bloody war, officially, however, they are both reluctant to disrupt Hafen trade and are attempting to curry favor with them.

At war with Thudia due to marital disputes in their gods (unoficially) and resource scarcity (officially), these kingdoms have been at war for nigh-on a century.

The god-king of Caenia is Ogreth, son of Caedus, is a great warrior who once slew an invading army by fighting single-handedly against them for 100 days and 100 nights.
